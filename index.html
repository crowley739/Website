Product Development

We offer services in almost every phase of product development. Our goal is to help you make your product function well and to also be easy to produce. Below are our main services that we offer.

  • Project Management, whether we are managing our own team, your internal team or outside consultants, our goal is to manage the project efficiently and effectively
  • Conceptual design, starting from just an idea
  • Detailed part design, including CAD, engineering drawings and tolerance analysis
  • Design verification and validation, including design input and output documentation and required testing
  • Design For Manufacturing (DFM) and Design For Assembly (DFA)
  • Prototypes for proof of concept and production equivalent prototypes to prepare you for tooling
  • Production support; once the product is ready to produce, we can help setup pilot lines and hand off manufacturing to higher volume manufacturing facilities
  • We partner with reputable quality engineers and regulatory professionals, so we can provide full support for medical devie development

Prototypes

Protist strives to make our clients ideas become reality. Sometimes it takes a fully functional or an aesthetic prototype to confirm form, fit and function of a product. Othertimes, it is necessary to create a prototype close to production equivalent to eliminate tooling risk and schedule delays. We offer several different prototype methods.

  • 3D Printing - FDM, SLA, DMLS and Polyjet
  • Urethane and silicone Casting
  • Aluminum and MUD base injection molding
  • Machining
  • Fully Functional Prototypes
  • Production Equivalent Prototypes
